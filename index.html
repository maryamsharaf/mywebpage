Picking the Perfect Pup

The perfect puppy, in my opinion, is the shy, small, and least likely to be picked since they are different
from the rest of the litter; their precious souls only want to be loved, and we humans must be there for
them. They'll exhibit their enjoyment once they've gotten acclimated to you. "They might only be there for a part of your
life, but to them you are their whole life."
